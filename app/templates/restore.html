Steps to migrate to a new Railway account:
  1. On old account: Go to Settings → click "Download Backup" to get the .zip
  2. Set up the app on the new Railway account with a volume mounted at /app/data
  3. Login to the new app (default: admin / admin123)
  4. Come to this page and upload the .zip from step 1
  5. Restart the Railway service for changes to take effect
